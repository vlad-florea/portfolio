About me

If you look through my galleries, you'll see everything from adrenaline-fueled sports to silent landscapes. While the subjects change, the goal remains the same: I am hunting for emotion.

Photography is my way of organizing the world. Sometimes that means capturing the peak velocity of chaos at a concert; other times, it means finding a singular moment of calm in nature. I've been obsessed with this balance-deconstructing angles and studying light-since I was a child developing my own black and white film.

For the last 15+ years, my "day job" in design and Quality Assurance has kept me tethered to screens. Photography is my escape into the analog reality of the world-a way to experience its "raw-ness" firsthand.
